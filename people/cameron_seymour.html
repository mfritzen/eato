Cameron Seymour, EI, SI

President/Founder

Cameron Seymour hails from Hillsboro, Indiana where he went to a small rural high school named Fountain Central. Cameron was accepted into the First Year Engineering program at Purdue University. After FYE, Cameron chose to pursue his Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering with a minor in Land Surveying. After graduating in May 2014, Cameron began his career practicing offshore surveying in the oil and gas industry out of Houston, Texas. In 2016, he brought his talents back to West Lafayette and started as a Project Designer for Schneider Geomatics. His proximity to Purdue allowed him to pursue his passion of mentoring and trying to help students navigate the experiences, triumphs, and tribulations of an engineering education. His goal for the mentoring program is to increase the graduation rate of engineering students from small rural schools and also create networking opportunities for career placement.
